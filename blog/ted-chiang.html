Ted Chiang - the author

Okay, Ted Chiang is hands down one of the most mind-blowing sci-fi writers out there, and if you haven't read his stuff yet, you're seriously missing out.


Often I find myself, yoyo-ing between either Dan Brown, Ted Chiang, or Stephen King. There's something to be said about consumption of entertainment that doesn't involve moving pixls, but involves embracing the written word.


Chiang's work has to be some of the most mind provoking sci fi stuff I've ever read. While other authors are pumping out novels left and right, Chiang's out here crafting these perfect little gems that'll have you thinking for weeks. His collections "Stories of Your Life" and "Exhalation" are basically masterclasses in how to do sci-fi right.


What sets him apart is how he weaves deep philosophical questions into genuinely compelling stories. He's asking the big questions about consciousness, free will, and what makes us human, but he's doing it through these incredible narratives that actually stick with you.

If you haven't checked out his work yet, do yourself a favor and dive in.
